WHAT ARE WE DOING?
TEENS & YOUTH OUTREACH
Teenagers from 8 to 18 years old can find good role models, learn new life skills and improve their intellectual and social abilities while receiving the practical and material support they need.
RESTORATION AND THERAPY
To enhance psycho-emotional, mental and social wellbeing of people, struggling with pain, fears, anxiety and other consequences of traumatic experiences in their lives.
HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE
In response to the desperate situations faced by vulnerable groups, BOL provides humanitarian assistance, which allow needy people cover their very basic needs such as food, clothing and hygiene items.
EARLY CHILD EDUCATION
Care for vulnerable women has arisen from an awareness of how deeply poverty and deprivation affect their lives, and subsequently the lives of their children, creating a vicious cycle of vulnerability.
WHO WE ARE?
OUR MISSION
To restore God's original intent for people’s holistic and harmonious development, true social justice and community transformation.
OUR VALUES
Professionalism and dedication Transparency and accountability Cultural relevance
OUR TEAM
Passionate and hardworking people from Moldova, who desire to see people together with their families and communities thriving. We put all our energy and efforts to bring hope, awakening and transformation in the lives of individuals, families and the communities.
